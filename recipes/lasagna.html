Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a type of pasta in the form of wide stripes that tends to be used to stack with meat and cheese in between each stack.

Ingredients
  • Ground Beef
  • Garlic Cloves
  • Whole Tomatoes
  • Tomato Paste
  • Cottage Cheese
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Lasagna Noodles
Steps
  1. Cook noodles according to package directions
  2. Cook meats and onions over medium heat for 8-10 minutes, add garlic
  3. Stir in tomatoes, tomato paste, water, sugar, 3 tablespoons parsley, and basil.
  4. In a small bowl, mix egg and cheese.
  5. Preheat over to 375 degrees. Spread meat sauce onto a baking dish and cover with 3 layers of noodles. Repeat layers twice. Top with remaining meat sauce and cheese.
  6. Bake, covered, 25 minutes. Bake, uncovered, 25 minutes longer or until bubbly. Let stand 15 minutes before serving.
